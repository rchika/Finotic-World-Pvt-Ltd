Comprehensive Job Description: Sales Executive (Personal Loan & Overdraft)

                   

                        Finotic World Pvt. Ltd. is rapidly expanding its footprint as a leading provider of comprehensive financial services, specializing in personalized loan and credit solutions for both individuals and small to mid-sized enterprises. We are actively seeking highly motivated, dynamic, and results-oriented individuals to join our high-performing sales team as a Sales Executive. This pivotal role is focused on driving the sales of our core products, primarily **Personal Loans** and **Overdraft facilities**. The ideal candidate will possess an unwavering commitment to achieving ambitious sales targets, a genuine interest in the financial market, and an exceptional ability to forge strong, trust-based relationships with potential clients. This is not merely a job; it is a career-defining opportunity to be a frontline ambassador for our brand, directly contributing to our organizational growth while maximizing your personal earning potential through our industry-leading incentive structure. Your success in this role is intrinsically linked to the financial empowerment of our diverse clientele.                    

                   

Core Responsibilities: Driving Financial Growth and Client Satisfaction

                   

The Sales Executive is entrusted with a wide range of duties that encompass the entire sales lifecycle, from initial lead generation and qualification through to successful disbursement and post-sales support. A detail-oriented approach combined with robust persuasive skills is essential for navigating the competitive financial landscape. Specific responsibilities are detailed below, emphasizing both quantitative and qualitative aspects of performance:

                                        
                           
  • **Target Achievement and Revenue Generation:** Consistently meet or exceed challenging monthly, quarterly, and annual sales targets for both Personal Loan and Overdraft products as defined by the sales management team. Focus on maximizing the loan value and ensuring portfolio quality. Proactively identify opportunities for product upselling and cross-selling to existing and prospective customers based on a thorough understanding of their financial profile and evolving needs.
    •                        
  • **Lead Management and Conversion:** Diligently handle, qualify, and prioritize a high volume of leads generated through various channels, including company-provided inbound enquiries, digital marketing campaigns, and self-sourced referrals. Implement effective lead follow-up strategies to maximize conversion ratios within specified service level agreements (SLAs).
    •                        
  • **Client Needs Analysis and Product Presentation:** Conduct comprehensive financial needs analysis for potential borrowers to accurately determine the most suitable financial product (Personal Loan or Overdraft). Articulate the value proposition of Finotic World’s offerings, clearly explaining interest rates, fees, tenure, collateral requirements (if applicable), and all terms and conditions in a transparent and ethical manner.
    •                        
  • **Application Processing and Documentation:** Guide clients seamlessly through the entire loan application process. Collect, verify, and scrutinize all necessary Know Your Customer (KYC), income, and financial documentation required for credit assessment, ensuring absolute adherence to Finotic World’s internal policies and regulatory compliance guidelines set by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and other relevant bodies. Address and resolve documentation deficiencies promptly to prevent application bottlenecks.
    •                        
  • **Credit Liaison and Coordination:** Act as the primary interface between the client and the internal credit and operations teams. Facilitate clear communication regarding loan sanction status, address any queries raised during the underwriting process, and proactively manage client expectations regarding processing timelines.
    •                        
  • **Market and Competitor Intelligence:** Stay fully informed on the prevailing financial market conditions, competitor products, pricing strategies, and latest regulatory changes that may impact sales efforts. Provide timely and actionable feedback to the management team to help refine product positioning and sales strategy.
    •                        
  • **Customer Relationship Management (CRM):** Meticulously update and maintain all client interaction records, sales activities, application statuses, and sales forecasts within the designated Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software. Ensure data integrity and use the system as a tool for pipeline management and strategic follow-up planning.
    •                        
  • **Ethical and Compliance Standards:** Operate with the highest level of professional ethics and integrity at all times. Ensure full compliance with all internal controls, company policies, and external financial regulations to mitigate risk and maintain the company's reputation for trustworthy service.
    •                        
  • **Continuous Professional Development:** Actively participate in all mandatory and optional sales training programs, workshops, and team meetings to enhance product knowledge, refine selling techniques, and stay ahead in a rapidly evolving FinTech environment.
    •                        
  • **Team Collaboration:** Collaborate effectively with colleagues across sales, marketing, and credit departments to ensure a cohesive and outstanding customer experience, working towards shared organizational goals.
    •                        
  • **Post-Disbursement Service:** Follow up with customers after loan disbursement to ensure satisfaction and gather testimonials, which can be leveraged for future referral-based sales. Address and escalate any post-sales grievances efficiently.
    •                        
  • **Sales Reporting:** Prepare and submit accurate and timely sales reports, forecasts, and pipeline reviews to the Sales Manager on a weekly and monthly basis, highlighting key performance indicators (KPIs) and outlining strategies for overcoming challenges.
    •                    
                   

Mandatory Qualifications and Eligibility Criteria

                   

To be considered for this high-impact Sales Executive role at Finotic World, candidates must meet the following minimum qualifications and eligibility requirements. We believe that a strong foundation in both academics and soft skills is crucial for success in the financial sales domain. We welcome both fresh graduates and experienced professionals who demonstrate exceptional drive and potential.

                   

I. Educational and Professional Qualifications:

                   
                           
  • **Education:** Minimum requirement is a Bachelor's Degree (any stream) from a recognized university. A background in Commerce (B.Com), Business Administration (BBA), or a major in Finance, Economics, or Marketing will be given preferential consideration.
    •                        
  • **Experience:** 0 to 3 years of total working experience.
    •                        
  • **Relevant Experience (Preferred):** Prior experience in direct sales, tele-sales, or field sales within the Banking, Financial Services and Insurance (BFSI) sector, Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs), or FinTech organizations is a significant advantage. Experience in selling loan products (Personal Loans, Business Loans, Home Loans) or credit cards will be highly regarded.
    •                        
  • **Freshers:** Highly energetic, articulate, and confident freshers who demonstrate an inherent aptitude for sales, strong negotiation potential, and a 'hunter' mindset are strongly encouraged to apply. Extensive training will be provided to promising candidates.
    •                    
                   

II. Essential Skills and Core Competencies:

                   
                           
  • **Communication Excellence:** Exceptional verbal and written communication skills in **English** and strong fluency in **Hindi** is mandatory for effective client interaction in the North Indian market. Ability to clearly articulate complex financial information simply.
    •                        
  • **Sales Acumen:** Proven ability to manage a sales pipeline, close deals, and consistently meet demanding sales quotas. Must be highly self-motivated, target-driven, and possess a proactive approach to business development.
    •                        
  • **Negotiation and Persuasion:** Strong negotiating skills with the capacity to overcome objections and effectively persuade prospective clients to choose Finotic World’s financial solutions over competitors.
    •                        
  • **Resilience and Adaptability:** Ability to maintain a positive attitude and persistent approach in the face of rejection, which is inherent in the sales cycle. Must be adaptable to changing market dynamics and evolving product offerings.
    •                        
  • **Technical Proficiency:** Basic working knowledge of MS Office Suite (Word, Excel) is required. Familiarity with CRM software (e.g., Salesforce, Zoho, or similar internal tools) is a distinct advantage.
    •                    
                   

III. General Eligibility and Work Environment:

                   
                           
  • **Legal Work Status:** Must have the legal right to work in India (Indian citizen or valid work permit holder).
    •                        
  • **Age Requirement:** Minimum 18 years of age.
    •                        
  • **Job Nature:** This is a full-time, office-based role requiring daily commute to our central office location in A-61, Block-A, Noida Sector 16. Candidates must be comfortable with a professional, dynamic, and target-driven work environment.
    •                        
  • **Work Schedule:** The role operates under standard business hours, with potential requirements for extended hours based on sales pipeline closing deadlines or business necessity. Punctuality and professional attendance are non-negotiable standards.
    •                    
                   

Unlocking Your Earning Potential: Salary and Incentives Structure

                   

At Finotic World, we firmly believe in rewarding performance excellence. Our compensation package is designed to be highly competitive, comprising a solid base salary supplemented by a virtually unlimited and aggressive incentive scheme. The structure ensures that top performers receive remuneration far exceeding the industry standard, making this a truly aspirational role.

                   
                           
  • **Base Salary:** A fixed monthly salary ranging from ₹15,000 to ₹25,000 per month, commensurate with the candidate's prior experience, skills, and performance during the interview process.
    •                        
  • **HIGH INCENTIVES:** The core value proposition of this role. Our incentive structure is percentage-based and tiered, offering exponential returns on exceptional performance. There is **no cap** on incentives. Successful Sales Executives are expected to earn multiple times their base salary in performance-related pay. The incentives are disbursed quickly, directly correlating effort to financial reward.
    •                        
  • **Career Progression:** Finotic World is committed to internal growth. Sales Executives consistently achieving and surpassing targets are placed on a fast-track promotion path to Senior Sales Executive, Team Leader, and eventually Assistant Manager roles within the first 12-24 months.
    •                    
                   

                        This comprehensive package, combined with our supportive work environment, robust training mechanisms, and a clear path for professional advancement, makes the Sales Executive position at Finotic World a premier opportunity in the Noida financial sector. We are looking for individuals who view sales as a profession, not just a job, and are ready to invest their energy and talent into a company that truly values their contribution. The sheer volume and granularity of the responsibilities outlined here underscore the critical nature of this role to our organization's future. Candidates must be prepared to absorb a substantial amount of product knowledge quickly and utilize advanced consultative selling techniques to convert complex financial leads into successful accounts. The expectation is that every Sales Executive acts as an independent business unit, managing their own pipeline and client relationships with utmost professionalism and a strong sense of ownership. A deep understanding of credit scores, CIBIL reports, debt-to-income ratios, and the general lending ecosystem is beneficial for a rapid start, although formal training will cover all technical aspects. The drive to succeed and a relentless pursuit of sales excellence are the key attributes that will define the most successful candidates in this role. We eagerly anticipate applications from those who are ready to make a significant, measurable impact from day one and capitalize on the high incentive potential.

    3.

    4.
