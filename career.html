Job Vacancy: Sales Executive

  • Company: Finotic World Pvt Ltd.
  • Location: A-61, Block-A, Noida Sector 16 (Near Metro)
  • Salary: ₹15,000 - ₹25,000 + HIGH INCENTIVES
  • Role: Sales Executive (Personal Loan Sales and overdraft)
  • We Seek: Energetic candidates (Fresher/Experienced) with excellent communication skills and a target-driven mindset.

Comprehensive Job Description

Finotic World Pvt. Ltd. is a fast-growing provider of personalized loan and credit solutions... This pivotal role is focused on driving the sales of our core products: **Personal Loans** and **Overdraft facilities**. The ideal candidate must be committed to achieving ambitious sales targets, possess a keen interest in the financial market, and demonstrate an exceptional ability to forge strong, trust-based relationships with clients. This role offers a significant career growth opportunity and maximizes personal earning potential through our industry-leading, high-incentive structure.

Core Responsibilities

  • **Sales Target Achievement:** Consistently meet or exceed demanding monthly, quarterly, and annual sales targets for Personal Loan and Overdraft products.
  • **Lead Management and Conversion:** Diligently handle, qualify, and prioritize high-volume leads generated through company inbound enquiries, digital campaigns, and self-sourced referrals.
  • **Client Consultation and Product Presentation:** Conduct thorough financial needs analysis and clearly articulate the value proposition of Finotic World’s offerings.
  • **Application Processing and Compliance:** Guide clients through the application process and ensure strict adherence to internal policies and RBI guidelines.
  • **Market Intelligence and Reporting:** Stay informed on market trends and meticulously update all sales activities within the designated CRM.

Mandatory Qualifications

I. Educational and Professional Qualifications:
  • **Education:** Minimum requirement is a Bachelor's Degree (any stream).
  • **Experience:** 0 to 3 years of total working experience.
  • **Freshers:** Highly energetic, articulate, and confident freshers are strongly encouraged.
II. Essential Skills and Core Competencies:
  • **Communication Excellence:** Exceptional verbal skills in **English** and strong fluency in **Hindi** are mandatory.
  • **Sales Acumen:** Proven ability to manage a sales pipeline, close deals, and consistently meet demanding sales quotas.
  • **Technical Proficiency:** Basic working knowledge of MS Office Suite (Word, Excel) is required.
