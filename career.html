Comprehensive Job Description: Sales Executive (Personal Loan & Overdraft)

Finotic World Pvt. Ltd. is a fast-growing provider of personalized loan and credit solutions for individuals and enterprises. We are seeking highly motivated, dynamic, and results-oriented individuals to join our sales team as a **Sales Executive**. This pivotal role is focused on driving the sales of our core products: **Personal Loans** and **Overdraft facilities**. The ideal candidate must be committed to achieving ambitious sales targets, possess a keen interest in the financial market, and demonstrate an exceptional ability to forge strong, trust-based relationships with clients. This role offers a significant career growth opportunity and maximizes personal earning potential through our industry-leading, high-incentive structure.

Core Responsibilities: Driving Revenue and Client Satisfaction

The Sales Executive manages the entire sales lifecycle, from lead qualification to successful disbursement and post-sales support. Key responsibilities emphasize both quantitative target achievement and qualitative client interaction:

  • **Sales Target Achievement:** Consistently meet or exceed demanding monthly, quarterly, and annual sales targets for Personal Loan and Overdraft products. Focus on maximizing loan value and seeking opportunities for product upselling and cross-selling based on comprehensive client needs assessment.
  • **Lead Management and Conversion:** Diligently handle, qualify, and prioritize high-volume leads generated through company inbound enquiries, digital campaigns, and self-sourced referrals. Implement effective follow-up strategies to achieve high conversion ratios.
  • **Client Consultation and Product Presentation:** Conduct thorough financial needs analysis for prospective borrowers to determine the most suitable financial product. Clearly articulate the value proposition of Finotic World’s offerings, transparently explaining interest rates, fees, tenure, and all terms and conditions in line with ethical and regulatory standards.
  • **Application Processing and Compliance:** Guide clients through the application process. Collect, verify, and scrutinize all necessary KYC, income, and financial documentation required for credit assessment, ensuring strict adherence to Finotic World’s internal policies and RBI guidelines.
  • **Credit and Operations Liaison:** Act as the primary interface between the client and internal credit teams. Facilitate clear communication regarding loan sanction status and proactively manage client expectations regarding processing timelines.
  • **Market Intelligence and Reporting:** Stay informed on market trends, competitor products, and regulatory changes. Provide timely feedback to management. Meticulously update all client interactions, sales activities, and forecasts within the designated Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software to ensure data integrity.
  • **Ethical Standards and Development:** Operate with the highest level of professional ethics. Ensure full compliance with all internal controls and policies. Actively participate in sales training programs to enhance product knowledge and refine selling techniques.
  • **Collaboration and Service:** Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (marketing, credit) to ensure an outstanding customer experience. Follow up post-disbursement to ensure customer satisfaction and generate referrals.

Mandatory Qualifications and Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must meet the following minimum qualifications and eligibility requirements. We seek a strong blend of academic foundation and crucial soft skills. Both fresh graduates and experienced professionals are encouraged to apply.

I. Educational and Professional Qualifications:

  • **Education:** Minimum requirement is a Bachelor's Degree (any stream) from a recognized university. Degrees in Commerce (B.Com), Business Administration (BBA), Finance, or Marketing are preferred.
  • **Experience:** 0 to 3 years of total working experience.
  • **Relevant Experience (Preferred):** Prior experience in direct sales or tele-sales within the Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) sector, NBFCs, or FinTech is a significant advantage. Experience selling loan products or credit cards is highly valued.
  • **Freshers:** Highly energetic, articulate, and confident freshers with an inherent aptitude for sales and negotiation are strongly encouraged. Comprehensive training will be provided.

II. Essential Skills and Core Competencies:

  • **Communication Excellence:** Exceptional verbal communication skills in **English** and strong fluency in **Hindi** are mandatory for effective client interaction in the North Indian market.
  • **Sales Acumen:** Proven ability to manage a sales pipeline, close deals, and consistently meet demanding sales quotas. Must be highly self-motivated, target-driven, and proactive.
  • **Negotiation and Resilience:** Strong negotiating skills to overcome objections and persuade clients. Must maintain a persistent and positive attitude in the face of rejection.
  • **Technical Proficiency:** Basic working knowledge of MS Office Suite (Word, Excel) is required. Familiarity with CRM software is an advantage.

III. General Eligibility and Work Environment:

  • **Legal Work Status:** Must have the legal right to work in India (Indian citizen or valid work permit holder).
  • **Age Requirement:** Minimum 18 years of age.
  • **Job Nature & Location:** This is a full-time, office-based role requiring daily commute to our central office location in A-61, Block-A, Noida Sector 16. Candidates must be comfortable with a professional, dynamic, and target-driven work environment.
  • **Work Schedule:** Standard business hours, with flexibility required based on sales pipeline closing deadlines.

Unlocking Your Earning Potential: Salary and Incentives Structure

Our compensation package is designed to be highly competitive, combining a solid base salary with a virtually unlimited and aggressive incentive scheme. We reward performance excellence:

  • **Base Salary:** A fixed monthly salary ranging from ₹15,000 to ₹25,000 per month, commensurate with experience and performance.
  • **HIGH INCENTIVES:** Our core value proposition. The incentive structure is percentage-based and tiered, offering exponential returns on exceptional performance. There is **no cap** on incentives, allowing top performers to earn multiple times their base salary in performance-related pay.
  • **Career Progression:** Sales Executives consistently achieving targets are placed on a fast-track promotion path to Senior Sales Executive, Team Leader, and Assistant Manager roles within 12-24 months.

This role demands individuals who view sales as a rewarding profession and are ready to invest their energy into a company that truly values contribution. Candidates must be prepared to quickly absorb product knowledge and utilize advanced consultative selling techniques. A conceptual understanding of credit scores, CIBIL reports, and the general lending ecosystem is beneficial for a rapid start. The unrelenting drive to succeed and a professional pursuit of sales excellence are the key attributes for the most successful candidates. We look forward to applications from those ready to make a significant, measurable impact from day one.
